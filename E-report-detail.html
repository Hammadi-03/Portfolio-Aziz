Advance Managing system

Rupio is a solution that offers financial literacy learning in a fun and interactive way. With rapid technological development,
many young people now prefer dynamic and non-monotonous learning methods, unlike traditional lecture-style learning. In addition, the tendency of young people to spend money just to fulfill their wants is also increasing.

Seeing this situation, we present an innovative idea to help the younger generation understand
management in a more enjoyable and lifestyle-relevant way.
